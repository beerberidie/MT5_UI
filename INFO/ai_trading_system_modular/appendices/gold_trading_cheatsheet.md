# Gold (XAUUSD) Trading Cheat‑Sheet (SAST)

- **Best hours:** 09:00–10:30 and 14:00–18:00.
- **Worst hours:** 00:00–07:00; 20:00 onwards.
- **Risk per trade:** $2–$3 (max $5 on $100 acct).
- **Preferred lot size:** 0.001 (if broker allows).
- **Targets:** Aim for 1:2 or 1:3 R:R.
- **Roadmap:** scale lots as equity grows (0.001 → 0.003 → micro 0.01).
